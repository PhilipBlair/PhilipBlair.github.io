‹ POSTER SESSIONS ›

We value things more if we understand them. Some thought like this got me thinking: Wouldnt it be nice to have a manual for modern museums that explains us what´s up with that complete white canvas in front of us? Well – to cut off all the professional art critiques right from the start – explaining everything up-front would make art broing, of course... But what about scientific content? Could there be a manual for some of the most beatiful and elegant ideas in the last 100 years? And how would it look like? This is the basic question for the Poster Sessions. I try to create something that works as a manual. A manual that is so appealing, people would want it to be hanging in their livingroom. In the following, explore some experimental ideas to my favorite topics in physics. In the future, text will be added and more pages to each topic will follow.
